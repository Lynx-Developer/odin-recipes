Salmon Smash Burger

Like a juicy beef burger, but with the freshy twist of the salmon's patty, this recipe is the best lunch for the seafood lovers, and in this post, you'll learn how to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of fresh salmon skinless filets

  • 1 large, beaten egg

  • 1 tablespoon all-purpouse flour

  • 1 (1 ounce) packet McCormickÂ® Burger Business by Tabitha Brown Seasoning Mix

  • 1 tablespoon grapeseed oil, plus more as needed

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Chop salmon or place in a food processor, and pulse a few times to chop.

  3. Combine salmon, egg, flour, dill, and burger seasoning in a bowl and mix gently.

  4. Scoop about 1 cup mixture and form into a ball for 4 to 5 balls.

  5. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.

  6. Place balls in the skillet, leaving plenty of space to smash them. Using a burger press or spatula press each salmon ball to a thin layer. You may have to work in batches. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side.

  7. Serve as desired in burger buns with your favorite toppings.
